The Fantasy Ashes

How it works

One squad per series, tied to your account — pick a team in My XI, no league required. Leagues (create or join under My Leagues) are an optional way to compare your team against friends; join several on the same series and it's the same squad on every one of their leaderboards. Your job is to manage it well as team news and form change.

Squad rules

  1. 14-man squad, picked once at the start of the series from the two contesting teams' pools.
  2. At least 5 players from each team — no lopsided raids on either pool.
  3. At least one wicketkeeper in your squad, and your starting XI must always include one.
  4. No budget cap — pick on judgement, not salary.
  5. From your 14, name a starting XI — the other 3 sit on the bench. Free to change anytime in My XI.
  6. Choose a Captain (2× points) and Vice-Captain (1.5× points) from your XI.

Between Tests

  1. Your starting XI (11 of your 14) can be changed freely in My XI at any time — it never counts as a transfer.
  2. Transfers — swapping a player out of your 14 for anyone else in the pool — are limited to 2 per Test, measured against your last locked squad. There's no limit at all before your first Test locks — chop and change your initial squad as much as you like right up to that first deadline. Committing just declares your intended squad; it's never blocked by the transfer count, but going over 2 (once the limit applies) without your wildcard is against the rules, so keep an eye on the count shown in My XI before each deadline.
  3. Once per series, arm your Wildcard to lift that 2-transfer limit. It's only actually spent if you commit while it's armed and your squad is still locked in that way when the deadline passes — arming and backing out costs nothing.
  4. Selection locks at 10:00am local time on the morning of Day 1 of each Test — see the countdown at the top of the page. Whatever you last committed becomes your new baseline for the next round of transfers.
  5. Captain / Vice-Captain can be reassigned anytime among your current XI, free of charge.
